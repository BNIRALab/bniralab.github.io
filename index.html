About BNIRA Lab

Where Neuroscience Meets Intelligent Systems

The Bio-Neural Intelligence and Research Advancement (BNIRA) Lab at Khulna University of Engineering & Technology (KUET) is a multidisciplinary research group dedicated to understanding the human nervous system through the lens of engineering and artificial intelligence.

Our work spans EEG/EMG signal analysis, brain–computer interfaces, neural decoding, and AI-driven diagnostics. We aim to develop translational tools that improve clinical outcomes and quality of life.

Current Research Directions

Interdisciplinary projects at the intersection of neuroscience, AI, and clinical translation

Motor Imagery BCI

Decoding motor intentions from non-invasive EEG to drive assistive and rehabilitative technologies for patients with motor impairments.

Cardiac Arrhythmia Detection

AI-powered ECG interpretation models for real-time arrhythmia classification, targeting deployment on low-resource embedded systems.
