Principal Investigator

Amit Dutta Roy

Assistant Professor (on leave)

Department of Biomedical Engineering, Khulna University of Engineering & Technology (KUET)

Graduate Fellow · Department of Biomedical Engineering, University of Minnesota Twin Cities

Amit Dutta Roy is an Assistant Professor (on leave) in the Department of Biomedical Engineering at Khulna University of Engineering & Technology (KUET), Bangladesh. He is currently a Graduate Fellow in the Department of Biomedical Engineering at the University of Minnesota Twin Cities, where he is pursuing his PhD. His research interests include neural engineering, neuromodulation, biosignal processing, and deep learning for biomedical applications.
