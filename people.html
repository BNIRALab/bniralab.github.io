Principal Investigator

ADR

Dr. Amit Dutta Roy

Assistant Professor

Department of Biomedical Engineering, KUET

Dr. Roy received his Ph.D. in Biomedical Engineering with specialization in neural signal processing and brain–computer interface systems. His research interests include EEG/EMG-based BCI, deep learning for clinical biosignals, and wearable neurological monitoring. He has authored papers in IEEE, Elsevier, and IOP Publishing journals and serves as a reviewer for several international journals.
