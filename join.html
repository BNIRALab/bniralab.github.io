How to Apply

To apply for any position at BNIRA Lab, please send an email to amitduttaroy@kuet.ac.bd with the subject line: "[Position Type] Application — [Your Name]".

1

Prepare your documents

CV/Resume, transcripts, brief statement of purpose (1 page), and contact info for 2 references.

2

Send your email

Include the subject line format above and attach all documents as a single PDF.

3

Initial screening

We will review applications on a rolling basis. Shortlisted candidates will be contacted for an interview within 2–3 weeks.

4

Decision

Successful candidates will receive an offer and onboarding details to join the lab.

What makes a strong application?

  • ✓ A clear, focused statement of purpose that matches our research areas
  • ✓ Evidence of prior research, projects, or relevant coursework
  • ✓ Strong academic record (especially in signals, math, and programming)
  • ✓ Any publications, preprints, or GitHub projects you have contributed to
  • ✓ Demonstrated curiosity and self-motivation
