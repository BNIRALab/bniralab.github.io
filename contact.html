Frequently Asked Questions

Are you currently accepting graduate students?

Yes, we are currently accepting applications for M.Sc. and Ph.D. students. Please review our Join Us page for details and send your application to Dr. Roy.

Can students from other universities join the lab?

Yes. We welcome visiting researchers and interns from other institutions on a rolling basis. Remote collaboration is also possible for specific projects. Please reach out with your background and areas of interest.

Do you collaborate with industry or clinical partners?

Absolutely. We actively seek clinical collaborations for dataset collection and validation, and are open to industry partnerships for translational research and product development. Please email us to discuss potential collaborations.

How quickly should I expect a reply?

We aim to respond to all inquiries within 3–5 business days. For graduate applications, shortlisted candidates will be contacted within 2–3 weeks of submission.
