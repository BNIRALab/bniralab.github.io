Projects

Funded research projects, thesis work, and open-source tools developed at BNIRA Lab.

Active Projects

5 Ongoing
Active 2025 – Present

NeuroAssist: Real-Time Motor Imagery BCI for Stroke Rehabilitation

Development of a low-latency, portable BCI platform using EEG for stroke patients undergoing upper-limb rehabilitation. Combines transformer-based neural decoding with robotic orthosis control.

EEG Motor Imagery Rehabilitation Robotics
Funded by: KUET Research Cell (Grant #BME-2025-01)  ·  Team: Dr. Roy, R. Sarker
Active 2024 – Present

CardioEdge: Wearable ECG Monitor with On-Device AI Arrhythmia Detection

Design and fabrication of a low-power wearable single-lead ECG patch with embedded TensorFlow Lite model for continuous, real-time cardiac arrhythmia classification without cloud dependence.

ECG Wearable STM32 TFLite
Funded by: Self-funded (prototype stage)  ·  Team: Dr. Roy, N. Kabir, R. Khan
Active 2024 – Present

ParkiNet: Early Parkinson's Disease Biomarker Extraction from Tremor & Gait

Multi-modal analysis combining accelerometer, EMG, and EEG data to extract clinically validated biomarkers for Parkinson's disease severity staging using graph neural networks and domain adaptation.

Parkinson's EMG GNN
Funded by: UGC Research Grant (in progress)  ·  Team: Dr. Roy, M. Hasan
Active 2025 – Present

SeizureGuard: Automatic Epileptic Seizure Detection from Ambulatory EEG

Collaboration with Dhaka Medical College to develop and clinically validate a deep learning model for automated seizure detection from ambulatory EEG recordings, with a focus on pediatric epilepsy.

Epilepsy Clinical EEG Pediatric
Collaboration: Dhaka Medical College  ·  Team: Dr. Roy, F. Akter
Active 2026 – Present

MindTrack: Cognitive Load Monitoring System for Students Using Passive EEG

A non-intrusive EEG-based system to monitor cognitive load and mental fatigue in students during academic tasks. Aims to provide real-time feedback to educators and learners to optimize learning sessions. Target deployment in KUET's smart classroom initiative.

Cognitive Load Passive BCI Education Tech EEG
Funded by: KUET Smart Campus Initiative  ·  Team: Dr. Roy, S. Islam, R. Khan

Completed Projects

3 Completed
Completed 2024

P300 BCI Speller with Adaptive Baseline Correction

A real-time P300-based character-spelling BCI system with adaptive artifact correction, achieving >85% ITR improvement over baseline. Formed the basis of S. Rahman's M.Sc. thesis.

Completed 2024

Low-Cost PPG-Based HRV Monitor

Open-source, battery-powered photoplethysmography device for heart rate variability analysis. Validated against clinical Holter monitors in a 30-participant study.

Completed 2023

EEG-Based Fatigue Detection for Drivers

SVM classifier on theta/alpha band power ratios to detect drowsiness in real time, demonstrated with a simulated driving dataset and custom EEG headband prototype.
